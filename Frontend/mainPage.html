Project Name

AI4Good Lab is a prestigious machine learning summer training program specifically designed for women and gender-diverse individuals across Canada. This program offers a comprehensive curriculum that encompasses the foundational concepts of machine learning, enabling participants to gain in-depth knowledge and hands-on experience. Throughout the program, attendees are encouraged to build meaningful relationships, collaborate on impactful projects with a purpose, and amplify the voice of the new generation in the field of technology. By fostering a supportive and inclusive environment, AI4Good Lab aims to bridge the gender gap in tech and empower underrepresented communities to contribute significantly to the advancements in machine learning and artificial intelligence.

Our team, comprised of six dedicated individuals from diverse backgrounds across North America, has embarked on a mission to address a pressing issue in our society: the food allocation problem. Recognizing the importance of providing affordable and accessible food options, we have designed a project that leverages machine learning to offer users more cost-effective choices of food available in their vicinity. Our innovative platform not only helps users find affordable groceries but also enriches their culinary experiences by providing a wide array of international recipes. By integrating recipe suggestions with corresponding grocery lists, we aim to simplify the cooking process and promote cultural diversity in cuisine. Our project is a testament to our commitment to using technology for social good and making a positive impact on the community.
